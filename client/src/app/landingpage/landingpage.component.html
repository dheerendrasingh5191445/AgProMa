Scrum At A Glance

What Is

Agile

Agile software development refers to a group of software development methodologies based on iterative development, where requirements and solutions evolve through collaboration between self-organizing cross-functional teams.

What Is

Project

A project is an individual or collaborative enterprise, involving research or design, that is carefully planned, usually by a project team, to achieve a particular aim A project may also be a set of interrelated tasks to be executed over a fixed period.

What Is

Scrum

A Scrum process is distinguished from other agile processes by specific concepts and practices, divided into the three categories of Roles, Artifacts, and Time Boxes. Scrum is a framework for managing software development

What Is

Management

Management (or managing) is the administration of an organization, whether it is a business, a not-for-profit organization, or government body.The term "management" may also refer to those people who manage an organization.
